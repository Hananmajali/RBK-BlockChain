What is it ?

RBK Blockchain Developer Bootcamp is an EXCLUSIVE one week intensive training program, it will be the Second experience in Jordan. The RBK Blockchain Developer Bootcamp is a highly selective, cooding intensive training program carried out over 6 days, included by a 2-days hackathon where participants will be required to create projects based on their learning.

What will you learn ?

The candidates who will be accepted to the course will have deep understanding of how the Blockchain system works and How does this new technology actually function.

Applicants Must Have

A Computer Science, Computer Engineering degree or any other related field.
At least 2 years of programming experience.
Experience & collaboration on a large code base.

Important Notes

1- Highly experianced candidates lacking formal education will also be considered for the program, based on their professional career path record and success in the pre-course process.
2- The cource will be delivered in English, so the candidates must have a very good English proficiency.

COURSE SYLLABUS

Ethereum


Ethereum, Smart Contracts.
Wallets, Crypto, Tokenization.
Distributed Ledger Technology.
Developing Blockchain Apps/API.
Open Source Tools / Algorithms.
ERC20 Wallet, ERC721 Wallet.
Solidity, Ganache, Web3.

COURSE STRUCTURE


PRE COURSE 2 WEEKS

An online course that will prepare you for the BootCamp and will cover the basics tools like Github and programming language that you will use through the program (JavaScript) to make you ready before starting this BootCamp.

TRAINING 1 WEEK

Here you will dive into: Tokenisation, Wallets, Crypto, Distributed Ledger Technology, Blockchain Structure, Hashing, Signing, Consensus, Solidity, Ganache, web3.
And as a part of this BootCamp you will create a cryptocurrency transaction.
